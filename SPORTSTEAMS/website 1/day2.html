Introduction

The Exeter Chiefs Corporate team aims to emulate the blockbusting performance of our 1st XV by matching their excellence on the field in all that we do off it. With hospitality and sponsorship opportunities available to suit all budgets, businesses can align themselves with the market leader on a seasonal or per-game basis, as we tailor a package to suit all aspirations. Be it a fantastic match-day for you and your guests, the best networking in the South West, through to worldwide exposure via our varied sponsorship options, the Chiefs are certainly the right fit for business looking to go the extra yard. On a match-day we take great pride in offering a premier experience when it comes to watching top flight rugby in a First Class environment. Our goal is to bring you unsurpassed experiences through the various corporate packages we have on offer. Away from the match, we are equally happy to ensure we develop a package that adds value, enhances your company profile and, ultimately, repeats the success of our club.
